ARTIST STATEMENT

The topic of my videos are mainly conceptual. My intention is to find the roots of transcendency. I examine different kinds of religions and the first spiritual rituals of humanity. I research the cognitive abilities of the human brain and examine the process of perception while experiencing sacred phenomena. The essence of my researches creates the topics of my art pieces.
