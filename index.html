ASMR Series - The Seven Deadly Sins 


2019, digital video on 17" digital frame

The video series reinterpret the Seven Deadly Sins made by Catholic culture in the context of the 21st century’s virtual society. The ASMR videos are artificially generated brain stimulations shared on online platforms, followed by millions. These affect the pleasure center of our brain with visual and audio stimuli that trigger the same processes in our brain as the experience of the Seven Sins. I use this ASMR video (mukbang, haul, makeup etc.) style to reinterpret each Sin.

PERSONAL PRAYER

2017, digital frame installation

My work is a prayer that is irrespective of cultures and religions. I created a poem that can be a general prayer for the human of the 21st century. I examined the sources of religions and art and tried to integrate this knowledge into a short poem that could summarize the essence of transcendental art.
“When the wind blows I’m gentle
When the rain falls I cry
When the storm comes I tremble
When the sun sets I die”
I created four video scenes about the four lines of the poem. These are influenced by nature because the basics of spiritual substances can be found in natural phenomena.

FREEDOM IS YOUR CROSS

2015, resin, projector

I project virtual skin through the resin copy of a human back. White words with meaning connects to the phenomena of liberalization appear on the surface. These start to cover each other until the full picture becomes white. This way the body can be released from its material skin.

GOING NOWHERE

2015, plastic boots, projector

In my video, I projected my feet through my transparent boots. But my shoes don't move. I stay in the same place during my journey. I combine the Eastern philosophy of motionless eternality with the Western linear way of thinking. The result represents the paradox nature of our world.

PARADOX OF DREAMING

2014, resin, projector

The video projected onto the plastic portrait visualizes a dream. The film is visible from the other side of the transparent mask because the projection is happening inside the plastic face. This is a transparent reproduction of my body. I am dreaming about my creation process because artistic creation and dreaming have the same unconscious roots. Opening and closing my eyes frames the stills and break of the dream.
